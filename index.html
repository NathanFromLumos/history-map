Echoes of History

An Interactive Historical Atlas

Hi, I'm Nathan, and I have ADHD. That usually means that instead of doing the housework, balancing my finances or exercising, I'm doing somethign else entirely. That's why this project exists.

History has always fascinated me. The rise and fall of empires, the battles that changed the world, the slow redrawing of borders across millennia. This is my small attempt to make that story somewhat explorable, to bring to life the rich and diverse history of our world in a way that's engaging for everyone.

Drag the timeline to journey through 4,000 years of human civilisation. Watch empires rise, borders shift, and the world we know slowly take shape.

I've got lots of other projects out there, as well as a substack where I infrequently write about how messy being human is.
